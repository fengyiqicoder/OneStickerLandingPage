How to Sync Notes from iPhone to Mac Using OneSticker

Syncing notes between your iPhone and Mac has never been easier with OneSticker. Follow these simple steps to ensure your notes are always up-to-date across your Apple devices.

1. Install OneSticker on Both Devices

Download and install OneSticker on your iPhone from the App Store and on your Mac from the Mac App Store. Ensure you're using the same Apple ID on both devices.

2. Create or Edit a Note on Mac

Open OneSticker on your Mac and create a new note or edit an existing one. Your changes will be automatically saved.

3. Access Your Note on iPhone

Launch OneSticker on your iPhone, and you'll see your notes synced automatically. Any changes made on your Mac will be reflected here.

With OneSticker, you can effortlessly create, edit, and sync your notes between your Mac and iPhone, ensuring your important information is always at your fingertips, regardless of which Apple device you're using.
