Such third party calendar service is a Non-OneSticker Service as that term is defined in the Agreement. You may also choose to integrate OneSticker Calendar with your third party conferencing provider. Such conferencing provider is a Non-OneSticker Service as that term is defined in the Agreement. Should you choose to authorize an integration with your conference provider, you will have the ability to add conferencing meetings to your calendar events, and to view and store any information associated with conferencing meeting invitations in OneSticker Calendar.
